## Main design decisions

1. Planning and finance are one combined Power.
2. Project Control Graph is the source of truth.
3. Skills read/write graph nodes; they do not own truth.
4. Historical data is benchmark only unless approved as evidence.
5. Intake Gate handles incomplete materials.
6. Interaction Steering controls user questions and confidence language.
7. Reports must be generated from graph-linked evidence, assumptions, decisions, derived calculations, or baseline version.
